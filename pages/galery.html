Useful information

    Advice on behavior towards wildlife:

  1. IF POSSIBLE, VISIT THE ANIMALS IN THEIR NATURAL HABITAT
    If you plan the most comfortable option of visiting a sanctuary or zoo, make sure it is accredited and professionally managed.
  2. SEE ANIMALS BEHAVE NATURALLY
    Snake charmers and monkey circuses may be alluring, but what happens behind the scenes can horrify you. To avoid encouraging these practices, find a respected elephant sanctuary or take a jeep ride to admire the elephants in their natural habitat.
  3. IT IS PROHIBITED TO CARRY
    This instruction might disappoint you, but you have to understand the mistreatment that surrounds facilities that offer experiences of active participation or close contact with animals.
  4. SPEND MONEY IN A SUSTAINABLE WAY
    If you are really concerned about animal welfare and wildlife sustainability, consider hiring an expedition group rather than organizing each activity yourself.
  5. RESPECT ANIMALS
    It is dangerous for you and can also cause problems for the animal. Also, of course, never feed the animals.
  6. ATTENTION WITH MEMORIES
    Be sure to carefully evaluate what you bring home from your wildlife adventure.
